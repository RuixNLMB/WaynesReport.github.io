More About Wayne

Hileman's twisted obsessions with genital mutilation and his brutal methods were often a result of his suppressed homosexuality. He would lure young men with his friendly demeanor and use their trust to take advantage of them, often causing them untold suffering before ultimately killing them. Despite his perverse methods, Hileman remained closeted, hiding his true identity from his family, friends, and community.

However, Hileman's double life as a married man to his wife, Laura, was not enough to conceal his true nature. His strange behavior and twisted fantasies often gave him away, and some of his classmates from high school recall that he spoke openly about his disturbing obsessions with other young men. Still, his pleas for help went unheard, and he continued to lead a double life as a married man and a brutal killer.
